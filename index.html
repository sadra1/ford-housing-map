Notes About the Data Portal

The Neighborhood Data Portal provides access to selected data on New York City and its neighborhoods, compiled and mapped by the Pratt Center for Community Development. Sources and notes for specific data can be viewed by clicking the “Details” button.

The NYU Furman Center for Real Estate and Urban Policy has generously shared data from Furman’s 2012 and 2103 State of New York City’s Housing and Neighborhoods reports.

Is there anybody else we want to acknowledge for data, or for help and advice? MATT HAMPEL

Development of the Neighborhood Data Portal has been generously supported by the Ford Foundation.
